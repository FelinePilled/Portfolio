Alleyway

Although it is a simple alleyway, this was one of the first backgrounds I had made for my Capstone animation. I have done a few background drawings before but none of them turned out exactly the way I wanted, but this alleyway was something I became proud of.

Dizzy

Dizzy is a cat character I created for fun, he is a representation of myself mixed with a more witchy archetype. This was one of the first pieces I did with a more complex pose and painting style, as I wanted to learn more about anatomy and painting skin.

Chillies on Mines Please

This drawing is a bit scary and hard to understand, it was based on a meme and was an inside joke with me and my friends. I included this because as goofy as this piece is, it shows off my ability to make anything creepy, which I often do because of my love for horror.

Flower Shop

Similar to the alleyway, this piece was made for my capstone project. I put a lot of detail into the flowers for no good reason. I don't often draw flowers, but my grandma has a major green thumb which sparked my love for flowers, so I wanted to portray them in the prettiest way I could.

Smiling Girl

As part of a school project, I decided I would draw all my classmates' characters. After about 3 headshot drawings I switched what my project goal was, but I ended up with some headshots I was proud of, such as this one. This is one of the first fully coloured drawings I have done in a long time.

Viking Father

Based on my father, this drawing is a personified version of him. My father is a 6"2 red-headed man, so I figured that portraying him as a cute chibi Viking would be fitting but also would contrast his actual image. My dad loved this drawing and had it put onto a sweater.
