Beep Logo

As long as I can remember, I have always gone by the name “Beep” online. Using this persona I made a logo to represent myself in the best way possible. In 2021 I adopted a black cat and he was one of the most important things in my life at the time, because of this I wanted my logo to be a black cat as I’ve also been described as having a “black cat” personality. As for the colours, the colour palette I usually follow with my wardrobe is black and red so I used those colours in my logo, with a yellow accent.

Purple Hippo Boutique

In college, I attended a business class for my multimedia diploma. During this class, we were given an assignment to find a local business with a logo that we thought needed a refresh. I ended up finding the Purple Hippo Boutique. Though their logo is alright, it’s a bit too detailed for a logo and the typography was very outdated. With this in mind, I created an entirely new Logo on Illustrator, keeping their Purple Hippo as the main component but adding a few other things such as a dress to portray that it was a boutique.

Worm Trix

In 2023 I got a button maker and from then on I designed a bunch of fake logos for coworkers, friends, family and teachers. One of my favourite designs was “Worm Trix” as it was a simple but silly idea. I made the design for a co-worker particularly fond of worms and skating, so I figured I’d combine them to make this little skating guy. Since it was a vector illustration, it was easy to size down to fit a 1.25cm button. I kept the design simple to prevent losing details when sized to such a small measurement.

Mushroom

While hand-designing my resume, I made a spot for an image of myself to go. However, I realized that my looks and appearance aren’t important to my job qualifications. I made a simple Mushroom to represent myself and fill that spot to keep the resume hierarchy consistent. This mushroom also shows off more of my digital design abilities.

Cyber Prodigy

During my first year of College, we were tasked to make a branding package for a made-up brand. We could choose from 4 different categories of themed brands. I went with the video game-themed brand and came up with this simple logo for the fake branding called “Cyber Prodigy”, I also made some other assets for them such as header images, gifs and patterns.

Mikan House

This logo was one of the first logos I had ever made. My skills and abilities have come a long way since this design but I still appreciate it for being a gateway to my growth. At the beginning of my schooling, a small group of coders came to my class requesting a design for their business. They are a small video game company in the workings and I’m happy to have created something cute for them as a change to my usual work.
